## Purpose
Support Business Analysts in gathering, documenting, and validating requirements for OctoAcme projects.

## Pre-Project Discovery
- [ ] Identify key stakeholders and their roles
- [ ] Understand business objectives and success metrics
- [ ] Review existing documentation and related projects
- [ ] Assess current-state processes and pain points
- [ ] Define scope boundaries and out-of-scope items

## Requirements Gathering
- [ ] Conduct stakeholder interviews
- [ ] Facilitate requirements workshops
- [ ] Document functional and non-functional requirements
- [ ] Capture edge cases and exception scenarios
- [ ] Validate requirements with stakeholders
- [ ] Prioritize requirements with Product Manager

## Requirements Documentation
- [ ] Create clear, testable acceptance criteria
- [ ] Document process flows and business rules
- [ ] Identify data requirements and constraints
- [ ] Map user stories to business objectives
- [ ] Link requirements to stakeholder needs
- [ ] Review documentation with technical team for feasibility

## Analysis & Impact Assessment
- [ ] Assess business impact of proposed solution
- [ ] Identify affected business processes and stakeholders
- [ ] Analyze cost vs. benefit
- [ ] Document assumptions and dependencies
- [ ] Identify integration points with existing systems
- [ ] Review regulatory or compliance considerations

## Validation & Sign-off
- [ ] Validate requirements completeness with stakeholders
- [ ] Review acceptance criteria with QA/Testing team
- [ ] Obtain stakeholder approval on requirements
- [ ] Confirm requirements align with project charter
- [ ] Ensure traceability from requirements to deliverables

## Ongoing Activities
- [ ] Participate in sprint planning and backlog refinement
- [ ] Clarify requirements during execution as needed
- [ ] Validate deliverables against acceptance criteria
- [ ] Support user acceptance testing (UAT)
- [ ] Document lessons learned and process improvements
- [ ] Update requirements based on feedback and changes
